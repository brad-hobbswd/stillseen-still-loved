Foreword

Still Seen, Still Loved

A companion for the silent, the misunderstood, and the brave.

Life rarely gives us a manual for navigating the complexities of love, identity, faith, and judgment. Many of us grow up learning lessons that are incomplete, misinterpreted, or harmful. Some of us are silenced, shamed, or forced to hide the deepest truths of our hearts, thinking we are alone in the struggle.

Still Seen, Still Loved is a guiding light for those moments. Bradley Wayne Hobbs does not write from a place of judgment or superiority. He writes from experience, observation, and deep faith, a faith that honors God's radical, inclusive love and the sacredness of every human heart.

Through these pages, you will find stories of courage, reflection, and spiritual insight. Biblical truths are presented as mirrors to reflect the heart, not as weapons to shame or control. Conversations are honored, and silence is respected.

Most importantly, this book affirms the truth that your life, your love, and your voice are sacred and valuable.

This is not simply a devotional. This is a companion for the silence, the closeted, the misunderstood, and the brave. A place where affirmation, hope, and courage meet, where God's love becomes tangible, and where you are reminded that you are never alone.

May these pages ripple outward, touching hearts, healing wounds, and empowering voices that have long been silenced.
