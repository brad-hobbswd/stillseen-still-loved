Prologue

For the Ones Who Feel Invisible

Psalm 34:18 "The Lord is near unto them that are of a broken heart."

To you who are reading this, whether in silence, in hiding, or in quiet reflection: this book is for you.

You have watched. You have listened. You have survived. You have carried the weight of judgment, misunderstanding, and fear. You have been told your love is wrong, your heart is sinful, or your identity is unworthy.

And yet, here you are alive, breathing, and still searching for truth, still longing for affirmation, still hoping for grace.

I know the world can feel heavy. I know family, friends, and even people of faith can hurt in ways that cut deeply. I know the fear of being fully seen is real.

Your life is precious. Your love is sacred. Your voice matters.

You are not invisible.

You are not less.

You are fully known, fully seen, and fully loved by God.

This book is not about judgment. This book is not about condemnation. This book is about walking with you through fears, questions, silences, and hidden places while reflecting God's unwavering love.

Through these pages you will find stories, reflections, Scripture, and truths designed to empower and remind you that even during your quietest moments, God has never abandoned you.

You are not alone.

You were never alone.

You will never be alone.
