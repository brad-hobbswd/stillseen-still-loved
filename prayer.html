A Meaningful Daily Prayer

Still Seen, Still Loved

Heavenly Father,

Thank You for seeing me even when the world does not. Thank You for loving me even when others misunderstand me. Thank You for my heart, my courage, and my capacity to love.

Lord, remind me daily of my worth, my strength, and my God given value. Help me stand in truth and walk in Your love without fear.

Give me discernment to recognize pride, judgment, and fear. Guide my heart toward wisdom, compassion, honesty, and courage.

Remind me that my life is precious. My love is sacred. My voice is powerful.

You created me fully and completely. I am enough.

Help me remember and carry this truth every day: I am seen. I am loved. I am enough.

Amen.

How to Use This Prayer
  • Recite aloud each morning as an anchor in God's love.
  • Memorize portions during seasons of fear or uncertainty.
  • Return to these words during moments of criticism or misunderstanding.
  • Allow this prayer to become a reminder of courage and belonging.
